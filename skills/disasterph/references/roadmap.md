# Roadmap

## MVP

- Map-first disaster dashboard
- Normalized incident model
- Source health and freshness display
- Filters by disaster type and region
- Selected incident details
- Initial alert engine behavior
- Responsive desktop sidebar and mobile bottom sheet

## V1.1

- Real ingestion from PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, and EONET
- Persistence, dedupe, and stale-source handling
- Region summaries and better filter combinations
- Alert subscriptions and outbound notifications

## V2 Stretch Ideas

- Historical analytics and incident timelines
- Regional heatmaps and trend views
- Confidence scoring and cross-source correlation
- Subscriber personalization and saved watch areas
- Deeper map layers and advanced geospatial overlays
